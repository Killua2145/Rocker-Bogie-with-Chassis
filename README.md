So this is a full scale 6 wheeled rocker bogey system with a proper differential. It is fully producable with CNC Machining, 3d printing and a bit of handiwork. It can be exported to URDF as well as Matlab. Motion Study in Soldiworks as well as simualtions on Ansys are also possible on this. Most of the parts are of aluminium 5051 grade. Some are of 6062 grade and some are mild steel. It is fully customisable.
